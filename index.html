Our Model



PLANNING


Our Team helps the farmers plan the cropping and the harvest pattern ensuring the best output.

Using our network, we make farming essentials available to farmers at affordable prices.


HARVEST


A weekly harvest schedule is prepared for farmers using Data analytics, maximizing profits.


COLLECTION


The harvest is collected directly from the fields, saving farmers the hassle of travelling to Mandis.


FULFILLMENT


The produce is sorted based on our quality standards and dispatched according to orders.
